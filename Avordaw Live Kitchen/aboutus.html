At Avordaw Live Kitchen, we bring you the authentic taste of world cuisines, where every dish tells a story of tradition, culture, and innovation.

  • Authentic recipes from around the world using traditional and modern cooking methods
  • Locally sourced fresh ingredients for maximum flavor
  • Expert chefs with decades of experience in global cuisines

Our journey began in the heart of Kolkata, where our founders grew up surrounded by the vibrant food culture of Bengal. From the bustling street markets filled with the aroma of freshly ground spices to the warmth of family kitchens echoing with laughter and the sizzle of traditional recipes, their earliest memories are steeped in culinary tradition.

Over the years, we've dedicated ourselves to preserving these culinary traditions while adapting them for modern palates. Our chefs have traveled across regions, learning age-old techniques from local artisans and infusing them with contemporary twists. Each dish is thoughtfully crafted, balancing authenticity with innovation, so that every guest can savor both nostalgia and novelty in every bite.
