At Avordaw Live Kitchen, we bring you the authentic taste of Bengal, where every dish tells a story of tradition and culture.

  • 100% authentic Bengali recipes using traditional cooking methods
  • Locally sourced fresh ingredients for maximum flavor
  • Expert chefs from Kolkata with decades of experience in Bengali cuisine

Our journey began in the heart of Kolkata, where our founders grew up surrounded by the vibrant food culture of Bengal. We've dedicated ourselves to preserving these culinary traditions while adapting them for modern palates. From the mustard-infused fish curries to the delicate sandesh desserts, every item on our menu is a celebration of Bengal's rich gastronomic heritage.

Why Choose Avordaw

In a world of fusion and fast food, we remain committed to preserving the authentic flavors of Bengal. Our kitchen follows traditional cooking techniques that have been perfected over centuries in Bengali households. We don't compromise on quality or authenticity - what you taste at Avordaw is exactly how these dishes were meant to be enjoyed.
